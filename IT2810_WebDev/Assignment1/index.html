My Abstract Art

A piece of art can be a simple splat of paint dispersed seemingly randomly on a sheet of canvas and still be worth billions. The brilliant thing about art is its inherent subjective worth. I specialize in simple star-shapes. Not unlike other artists, my works are of questionable quality. This is the essence of art.

Flexbox has been used as there are only two div's in need of styling with position and layout (those containing the SVG and Canvas Elements). CSS Grid would have been more appropriate with several other div's - with need of styling with both rows and columns - source.

Canvas is defined in an external js-file. jQuery is used to invoke actions on the Canvas, such as hover.
SVG is defined inline in the HTML due to the simplicity of the element. The interactive part is ensured by a simple css-command. This has the advantage of targeting the polygon itself, instead of the entire element. jQuery is imported in the script tag below, and used in index.js to simplify all the actions (.ready and .hover) on the elements in the html-file.

The website is tested in both Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome - two of the most used browsers. Functionality is tested, in addition to checking the console in each browser for errors or warnings. Some of the information used here was fetched from this tutorial , this article and MDN documentation.
